Advanced Security Solutions

At SKY POINT, we specialize in the design, installation, and maintenance of comprehensive CCTV security and surveillance systems. Our solutions provide reliable monitoring for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

A Closed-Circuit Television (CCTV) system is a video surveillance network that captures and transmits video signals to a limited, private set of monitors or recording devices, rather than broadcasting them publicly.

Our expert team ensures that your CCTV system is tailored to your specific security needs, providing optimal coverage and functionality for your property.

Our CCTV Solutions

We offer a wide range of CCTV systems to meet diverse security requirements

Analog CCTV Systems

Traditional yet reliable analog systems with DVR recording, suitable for basic surveillance needs with cost-effective solutions.

HD CCTV Systems

High-definition cameras providing crystal clear images with superior resolution for detailed monitoring and identification.

IP Network Systems

Advanced IP-based systems with network connectivity, remote access, and superior scalability for modern security needs.

Wireless CCTV Systems

Flexible wireless solutions that eliminate the need for extensive cabling while maintaining high-quality video transmission.

Mobile Surveillance

Remote monitoring solutions that allow you to view your property from anywhere using smartphones, tablets, or computers.

Night Vision Systems

Advanced infrared technology for 24/7 surveillance with clear imaging even in complete darkness or low-light conditions.
