Comprehensive MEP Solutions

At SKY POINT, we specialize in the design, installation, and maintenance of Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) systems within buildings and infrastructure projects. Our integrated approach ensures all building systems work together seamlessly for optimal performance.

MEP Contracting involves the design, installation, and maintenance of Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems within buildings and infrastructure projects. These systems are vital for a building's functionality, safety, comfort for occupants, and energy efficiency.

In regions like the UAE with extreme climates and rapid development, reliable and efficient MEP systems are critical for optimal building performance, energy efficiency goals, and compliance with local regulations.

MEP Systems Overview

Understanding the core components of Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems

Mechanical Systems

Primarily focuses on Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning (HVAC) systems, crucial for climate control and indoor air quality, along with other mechanical equipment like elevators and fire safety systems.

  • HVAC System Design & Installation
  • Ventilation & Air Quality Control
  • Energy Recovery Systems
  • Building Pressurization
  • Smoke Control Systems
  • Mechanical Equipment Rooms

Electrical Systems

Encompasses power distribution, lighting design, backup power solutions, and ensuring overall electrical safety and functionality within a structure.

  • Power Distribution & Panels
  • Lighting System Design
  • Emergency & Backup Power
  • Electrical Safety Systems
  • Data & Communication Wiring
  • Energy Management Systems

Plumbing Systems

Deals with water supply, drainage, sewage systems, and sometimes includes specialized systems like irrigation or firefighting plumbing.

  • Potable Water Systems
  • Sanitary Drainage Systems
  • Storm Water Management
  • Gas Piping Systems
  • Water Conservation Systems
  • Medical Gas Systems
