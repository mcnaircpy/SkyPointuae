Interior vs Exterior Design

Understanding the distinct approaches to designing indoor and outdoor spaces

Interior Design

Interior decoration involves enhancing the appearance of the indoor spaces within a building, focusing on furniture, colors, textiles, and other decorative elements to create a cohesive and inviting atmosphere.

  • Space planning and layout optimization
  • Color scheme selection and coordination
  • Furniture selection and arrangement
  • Lighting design and implementation
  • Material and finish selection
  • Decorative accessories and artwork

Exterior Design

Exterior decoration focuses on the visual appeal of a structure's outside appearance, including landscaping, building materials, outdoor lighting, and designing functional outdoor spaces like patios and gardens.

  • Building facade design and materials
  • Landscape design and planting schemes
  • Outdoor living space creation
  • Exterior lighting design
  • Hardscape elements (paths, walls, etc.)
  • Entrance and gateway design
